Taking the Citizenship Test

 

•  Keep checking for the invitation to your respective test.

•  Often we send the invitation within the one week following the date that you inform us that you have received the AOR letter or the email.

•  In some circumstances, we confirm that the application that you have submitted is comprehensive, and then we forward the AOR.

•  This is especially so if you have not yet received your AOR, you should then check if it is still expected soon.

•  If the email has been included in the application then an email invitation will be sent either to the applicant or his/her representative.

•  If you have not received our emails, please check your junk or spam folder with the following suffix: “@cic. gc. ca. “

 

Procedures that are followed while taking a test

You can only take the test once you’ve received an invitation, and nobody quite knows whose invitation is whose. First of all, it depends on the type of test you are going to complete because the practice can be performed in any form.

If you decide to continue the test through the Internet the result of the test in this case is as follows. 

 

•  Do not forget to refresh in the tutorial on the online test.

•  Know what is expected of you in regard to the Test for instance

    •  Those IDs the form of identification which is necessary 

     •  application interfaces and devices which have the capability to cooperate

     •  The right place of sitting for the test

•  After receiving the invitation, you don’t need to take the Test immediately because you are welcome to do it whenever you want to.

•  The test has to be completed in 30 days.

•  During the 30 day period one is allowed to attempt the online test and a maximum of three times is allowed. 

 

Regardless if you decide to take the test in the Microsoft Teams application or in a physical environment

If you request special arrangements, it may be possible to be assessed on Microsoft Teams for the Test or in another location. For further information, please see the information which was given to you in the letter inviting you for the test. In case you do not succeed, you can attempt the test 2 other times.

The kind of test that you failed will dictate what should happen next. Please answer the provided queries below in order to have a deeper understanding.

Following the Test: The latter is followed by outcome as well as future actions.

After the test is over, the result for the test will be given to you. In one way or the other, if you pass the test or if you fail it, then you will do something or the other.

 

Result of a test

•  The way you get the result depends on the test you took either online or on Microsoft teams or even a physical environment.

  •  If you pass the Test which is a key test for the faculty members.

•  The pass rate for the test is 75% which can only be achieved by the learner if they give at least 15 out of 20 correct answers.

•  There could be circumstances where a person could be interviewed with the citizenship officer every now and then.

•  For the record we will invite you for the citizenship ceremony.

 

But if you do not pass the test

•  There are three chances to complete the test and they could be taken online, as well as using the Microsoft Teams application, or in a physical class.

•  Retaking the test you are likely to fail it three times then you receive an invitation for a meeting with a citizenship officer. These are questions like those you are given during the citizenship test structured orally during the hearing.

•  When you successfully go through the hearing, we shall move to the next step of processing of your application.

•  If you fail the hearing Test you could be referred to a different doctor without the need for a new prescription.

•  If your application is rejected then you are out.

•  However, if you still desire to be a citizen, you will be required to fill a new application as well as the costs shall be required again.
